RENOWNED JOURNALIST

Akhanda Bhandari

Akhanda Bhandari is a renowned journalist and editor who currently serves as the Editor-in-Chief of Annapurna Post, one of Nepal’s most popular national dailies. He is widely recognized in Nepal for his insightful write-ups on social issues through his widely-read column, ‘Voice of the voiceless’. Akhanda’s articles cover a wide range of topics including good governance, social and political reforms, human rights, and democracy, and have had a significant impact on Nepali society. Prior to joining Annapurna Post, Akhanda served as the Creative Editor for Kantipur national daily and coordinated the gulf edition of Kantipur weekly based in Qatar. In addition to his journalistic work, Akhanda is also an accomplished author and has written three books, including Malaya Express, Samacharko Bhasa, and Dolpo Buddha. He has been recognized for his excellent contribution to national service and public interest through journalism and was awarded by the President of Nepal for his outstanding achievements.

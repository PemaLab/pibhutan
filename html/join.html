BECOME A MEMBER

Peace Initiative Bhutan is a non-partisan advocacy organization that is driven by its membership and is based in the United States of America. We recognize that transformative change in Bhutan is essential and solutions to the ongoing political problems that have caused one-sixth of Bhutanese citizens to become refugees requires building a global organization that advocates for peacebuilding and reconciliation. Our mission is to promote peace and happiness through compassion, non-violence, and respect, inspiring individuals and building a harmonious Bhutanese society through education, research, and community engagement. By becoming a member of our organization, you can help achieve our mission to sustainable peace and reconciliation in our Bhutan.

As a member of Peace Initiative Bhutan, you will receive invitations to unique members-only events and our monthly updates, annual reports, and information about upcoming actions that you can take to make a difference. We encourage you to join our general membership by paying the $100 annual fee or the $1000 annual executive membership and become an integral part of our organization.

If you would like to contribute to our organization, we invite you to do so here.

SOCIAL JUSTICE ADVOCATE

Sudarshan Pyakurel

Sudarshan Pyakurel is a highly respected community leader and social justice advocate, as well as a former refugee from Bhutan. He currently serves as the Executive Director of the Bhutanese Community of Central Ohio (BCCO), where he works tirelessly to promote equity, advocacy, and empowerment for refugees and immigrants in the state.

Sudarshan’s commitment to mental health and trauma-informed care is also a key part of his work. He is a nationally certified Mental Health First Aid instructor and a member of Trauma Responsive Care through the CARE Coalition. Additionally, he serves on the steering committee for the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Service Administration (SAMHSA) for Region 5, further cementing his expertise in this area.

In recognition of his leadership and dedication, Sudarshan was appointed to the State New American Advisory committee by Ohio Governor Kasich in 2018 and was reappointed by Governor DeWine in 2021. He is also a Migrant and Refugee Leadership Academy Fellow, demonstrating his commitment to developing and nurturing leadership skills among the next generation of advocates.

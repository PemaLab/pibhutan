FOUNDER PRESIDENT

Suraj Budhathoki

Suraj Budathoki is a Refugee Congress Honorary Delegate from New Hampshire and an advocate for the rights and interests of refugees and immigrant communities. He co-founded Building Community of New Hampshire to provide support for these populations and later became its Executive Director. Suraj’s leadership and advocacy have earned him recognition, including nominations for the American Council of Young Political Leaders and being named to the Union Leader’s 40 Under 40 Class. He is a political enthusiast and worked as the deputy political director at Bernie Sanders’s presidential campaign in New Hampshire. Suraj is currently pursuing a Doctoral degree in Transformative Social Change at Saybrook University, and his research focuses on transforming conflicted societies into harmonious ones through humanistic values. He is a writer and commentator, with published works in national and international media outlets. His upcoming book, ‘Crossline to Bhutan,’ explores the need for peace and reconciliation in Bhutan.”

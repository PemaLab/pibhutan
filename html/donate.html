Donate Us

The Peace Initiative Bhutan aims to create a national and global network of Bhutanese diaspora who are dedicated to building a peaceful and positive future for Bhutan. Our goal is to bridge the gap between the Bhutanese government and those in exile, find common ground, and promote justice and healing through compassion, kindness, and forgiveness.

We invite you to contribute to our cause by supporting our discussion events in target countries and states, as well as advocating for our mission on both national and global levels. For any questions or inquiries, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us via contact form.
